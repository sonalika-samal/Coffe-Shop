Arabica Coffee Beans

1399/-

The Arabica Coffee Beans is the most dominant cultivation of coffee beans these days and it is said that these are the first species of coffee to have been cultivated. Arabica Coffee is one of the most commonly served coffee in popular coffee shops, like Starbucks.

Liberica Coffee Beans

1375/-

The Liberica Coffee plant produces those larger, irregular-shaped cherries compared to Arabica plants. The fruits also possess a floral and fruity aroma but once it is made into coffee, the Liberica Beans have a full-bodied, woody taste.
