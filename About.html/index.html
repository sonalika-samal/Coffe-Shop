About Us

Welcome to The Golden Beans Coffee, where passion meets perfection in every cup. Established in 2021 by Sonalika Samal, our coffee shop is dedicated to providing a world-class coffee experience both in-store and online.

At The Golden Beans Coffee, we believe in the artistry of brewing and the richness of flavor. Our commitment to quality extends from the finest coffee beans sourced globally to the hands that carefully prepare each cup.

Whether you're a coffee enthusiast seeking a cozy atmosphere or a connoisseur looking to explore exceptional coffee beans, we have something special for you.

Our Offerings

Explore a world of flavors with our carefully curated selection of world-famous coffee beans. We take pride in offering a diverse range of high-quality beans that you can enjoy at home.

Visit our physical store to experience the aroma and taste of our freshly brewed coffee. Alternatively, browse our online store and have the finest coffee beans delivered to your doorstep.
